Timeline of London

43 AD

Roman Foundation

Londinium established as major Roman port
First stone bridge over Thames Forum and Basilica complex 3km defensive wall (London Wall) Population: ~30,000

1066

Norman Conquest

William the Conqueror's legacy: White Tower construction begins (1078) First royal coronation at Westminster Abbey (1066) Magna Carta signed (1215) Population reaches 80,000 (1300)

1500s

Tudor London

Renaissance transformations: First public theaters (1576) Royal Exchange founded (1565) Population triples to 200,000 Shakespeare's Globe built (1599)

1666

Great Fire of London

4-day catastrophe: Destroyed 13,200 houses 87 parish churches lost St Paul's Cathedral rebuilt by Wren First fire insurance (1681)
